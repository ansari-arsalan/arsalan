Lessons From the Ford Foundation

January 2025

During my time with the Ford Foundation I gained a new appreciation for how philanthropy can advance social justice. Working alongside advocates highlighted the role of technology in amplifying marginalized voices and pushing for more inclusive public policy. The experience also underscored that partnerships between nonprofits and government are crucial to tackling systemic inequality.

Beyond the day-to-day work, the foundation showed me how storytelling and community engagement remain powerful tools for change. Philanthropy isn't a replacement for public institutions, but it can help spark innovation and empower people at the grassroots level.
